Blood Test

Blood tests analyze blood samples to assess various health indicators, providing valuable insights into overall well-being and specific conditions.

Ultra Sound

Ultrasound uses sound waves to create images of internal organs, aiding in the diagnosis of issues such as tumors or abnormalities.

Biopsy

Biopsy involves extracting tissue samples for microscopic examination, helping to confirm or rule out potential diseases and guide treatment decisions.
